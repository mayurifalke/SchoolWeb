Bus Facility



Transport Facility
Godavri Public School has a transport facility which comprises of a fleet of well-maintained buses, equipped with Global Positioning System [GPS] real time tracking and CCTV cameras as per government guidelines. The buses have been fitted with speed governors too. Each bus trip has a responsible school attendant on board to ensure safety of children. The bus staff have been sensitised to the school’s stance on student safety.
